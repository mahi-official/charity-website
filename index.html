Aspirations and Guiding Principles
Defining Our Mission and Vision

In 2020, I initiated a transformative journey to impact the lives of underprivileged students in Delhi, India. I recognised their lack of career guidance and resources and established "Helping U Secure Knowledge" (HUSK), a registered NGO. Leading a diverse team of professionals in finance, business, law, and entrepreneurship, we aimed to empower these students.

Our mission was clear: enlighten students in government schools across Delhi about diverse career options. We organized seminars discussing in-demand and overlooked career paths, aiding students in making informed decisions. Our commitment extended beyond knowledge sharing. We provided tailored school support, donating essential resources like books, shoes, and sports or musical equipment. Through HUSK, we imparted knowledge and instilled hope and opportunity in those who needed it most.
